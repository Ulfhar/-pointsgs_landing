About Points Game Studio

Who We Are

Points Game Studio is an independent mobile game development team passionate about creating magical, story-rich puzzle platformers. As an indie game studio, we focus on original, emotionally engaging experiences that spark imagination and wonder.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop mobile games that blend compelling storytelling, artistic worldbuilding, and meaningful mechanics. We aim to craft experiences that leave a lasting emotional impact and invite players into beautifully immersive fantasy realms.

Our First Game: Far-Far Forest

Far-Far Forest is our debut title — a fairy tale puzzle game set in a forgotten magical forest. Designed for Android, the game combines handcrafted 2D levels, atmospheric music, and deep lore with intuitive gameplay. Players uncover ancient secrets by solving puzzles, collecting glowing fireflies, and exploring mysterious ruins.

Our Development Approach

  • Story-First: Each game begins with a strong narrative foundation rooted in myth and emotion.
  • Artistic Excellence: Visual design is core to player immersion — every screen is a crafted scene.
  • Meaningful Mechanics: Gameplay supports story and mood, not just challenge.
  • Indie Values: We value freedom, originality, and building a community of engaged players.
