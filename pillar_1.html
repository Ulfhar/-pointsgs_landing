Overview

A Living Forest That Grows With You

Lumen Grove is a lore-driven cozy forest farming and progression game set in a magical world shaped by memory, restoration, and quiet change. You care for a magical grove, help it grow, and restore its forgotten greatness.

Core features

  • Cozy farming inside a persistent forest hub
  • Layered progression systems built around growth and upgrades
  • Decoration and customization through themed decor packs
  • Puzzle platformer module connected to narrative exploration
  • LiveOps-ready world designed for long-term evolution

Progression layers

  • Immediate feedback through harvest loops and short upgrades
  • Mid-term goals through plant tiers and new unlocks
  • Long-term forest restoration through Enchanted Oak progression

Core loop

  • Buy and place plants
  • Grow plants to maturity
  • Collect resources
  • Complete orders
  • Invest in the Enchanted Oak
  • Decorate the grove
  • Unlock new layers of the world
