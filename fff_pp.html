Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15/01/2025  |  Last Updated: 20/06/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Bogdan Kagitin ("developer," "we," "us"), an individual developer based in Estonia, collects, uses, and shares information when you use the mobile game Lumen Grove (the "Game").

1. Data Controller

Bogdan Kagitin
Estonia
Email: info@points.gs

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected

  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • Anonymous user identifier (assigned at first launch; not linked to any name or email)
  • Country or region (inferred from your connection)
  • Gameplay data (in-game actions, progress, session length)
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID) — only with your consent where required by law

2.2 Data from Third-Party SDKs

We integrate the following services, which collect data on our behalf and for their own purposes:

Service Provider Purpose
Unity Analytics Unity Technologies, Inc. Gameplay behaviour, performance analysis
Google AdMob Google LLC Displaying in-game advertisements
Unity Cloud Save Unity Technologies, Inc. Storing game progress and settings

Unity Technologies operates as an independent data controller for data collected through Unity SDKs and processes it under their own Privacy Policy: unity.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Google LLC operates as an independent data controller for data collected through Google AdMob and processes it under Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

2.3 Anonymous Authentication

The Game assigns a persistent anonymous session ID at first launch. This ID is not linked to your name, email, or payment information. It is used solely to save and restore your progress via Unity Cloud Save.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Purpose Legal Basis
Crash reporting, game stability Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f))
Delivering purchased IAP content Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b))
Advertising and behavioural analytics Consent (Art. 6(1)(a))

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Game
  • To display in-game advertisements
  • To store and sync your game progress across devices
  • To analyse gameplay behaviour for development purposes
  • To respond to support requests

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is rated 3+ and is accessible to children of all ages. We take children's privacy seriously and comply with applicable law, including GDPR and, where applicable, COPPA.

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (name, email, address) from any user, including children
  • The anonymous identifier assigned by the Game contains no personal details
  • Google AdMob is configured with child-appropriate settings (tagForChildDirectedTreatment = true for users under 16). Personalised advertising based on behavioural profiling is not enabled in child-directed contexts
  • Parents or guardians who believe their child's data has been collected inconsistently with this Policy should contact us at info@points.gs — we will promptly investigate and delete such data if confirmed

6. Third-Party Services and Independent Data Collection

Our third-party SDK providers collect data under their own privacy policies and terms, independently of our instructions. We are not responsible for their data practices.

Unity Technologies may collect device identifiers and usage data for their own analytics, fraud prevention, and product improvement. We encourage you to review their Privacy Policy at unity.com/legal/privacy-policy.

7. International Data Transfers

Unity Technologies is based in the United States. Data processed through Unity SDKs may be transferred to the US or other countries outside the EEA. Such transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission. See Unity's Privacy Policy for details.

9. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of data we hold
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

Submit requests to info@points.gs. We respond within 30 days.
You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Estonian Data Protection Inspectorate: aki.ee

10. Analytics and Advertising Opt-Out

You may opt out of analytics and personalised advertising at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • Or contact us directly at info@points.gs

11. Consent Records

Where we rely on your consent to process data, your acceptance is recorded via Unity Cloud Save solely to confirm agreement and ensure compliance. You may withdraw consent at any time — withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing that occurred before withdrawal.

12. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications about game updates. You can disable them at any time in your device settings.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy at any time. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect changes. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance.
