Overview

What is Lumen Grove?

Lumen Grove is a lore-driven cozy forest farming and progression game set in a magical world shaped by memory, restoration, and quiet change. You care for an enchanted grove, help it grow, and restore its forgotten greatness — one firefly at a time.

FAQ

What kind of game is Lumen Grove?

A lore-driven cozy forest farming and progression game with a short puzzle-platformer module connected to the narrative.

What's the core gameplay loop?

Plant and place plants, grow them, collect resources and fireflies, invest in the Enchanted Oak, decorate the grove and unlock new layers of the world.

Where can I play it?

Currently in early access on Android via Google Play. iOS is on the long-term roadmap.
